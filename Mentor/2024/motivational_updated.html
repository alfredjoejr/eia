Project Overview

EIA organizes Motivational Seminars with the support of its undergraduate members to inspire and motivate school students by sharing effective study methods and techniques. The aim is to boost their confidence, broaden their perspectives, and encourage them to choose the science stream.

Benefits of the Motivational Seminar

The Motivational Seminar provides an excellent platform to inspire students and members to unlock their potential and stay focused on their goals.

By listening to experienced speakers and success stories, participants gain valuable life lessons and practical strategies for personal and academic growth.

Impact Among Participants

The seminar has a powerful impact on participants by igniting a sense of purpose and determination. It encourages them to step out of their comfort zones, overcome self-doubt, and strive for excellence.

The interactive nature of the seminar fosters active engagement, and many participants leave with a renewed sense of motivation and clarity about their goals.
