Benefits of Arduino Technology

Arduino provides an affordable and accessible platform for learning electronics and programming. It encourages critical thinking, problem-solving, and creativity by allowing students to build interactive projects that help them understand real-world applications of STEM concepts. Nowadays, Arduino is also widely used in university-level projects, making knowledge of this technology essential for students.

Impact among teachers

Teachers have expressed appreciation for the workshop, as it enhances their teaching methods and introduces new tools to engage students in technology-based learning.

It also helps them stay updated with modern educational practices and inspires curriculum integration.
