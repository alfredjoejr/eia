Events

  • Teaching Project


    Through this project, our EIA members & volunteers mentor and teach science stream subjects to G.C.E. Advanced Level students, especially those from rural areas. The project aims to uplift the educational standards of students in the Batticaloa and Ampara districts.

  • Arduino Workshop


    Through this project, our EIA members introduce Arduino-based programming and electronics to school students, helping them gain hands-on experience in STEM education. The project aims to spark creativity and innovation among students.

  • Physics Practical Workshop

    We guide school students in performing key physics practicals, enhancing their hands-on skills and conceptual understanding. The project aims to bridge the gap between theory and practice for students, especially those with limited laboratory access.

  • Car Wash

    We organize car wash campaigns to raise funds for educational and community service activities. The initiative not only supports EIA’s projects but also encourages teamwork and volunteerism

  • Social-Related Project

    EIA carries out at least one social-related project each year to support and uplift the community. These projects address local needs such as environmental protection, animal welfare, or social awareness.

  • EIA Cricket Fiesta

    EIA organizes its Annual Cricket Tournament to encourage unity, teamwork, and sportsmanship among its members. The aim is to create a fun and engaging platform to strengthen the bonds between seniors and juniors.

  • Chessathon

    EIA organizes its Annual Chess Tournament to encourage strategic thinking and concentration among school students. The aim is to prepare them to participate in national-level chess competitions.

  • Blood Donation Camp

    EIA organizes its Blood Donation Camp to support patients in need and raise awareness about the importance of donating blood.

  • Motivational Seminar

    EIA organizes Motivational Seminars with the support of its undergraduate members to inspire and motivate school students by sharing effective study methods and techniques. The aim is to boost their confidence, broaden their perspectives, and encourage them to choose the science stream.

  • Annual Get-Together

    EIA organizes its Annual Get-Together to strengthen the bond among members through fun activities, sharing sessions, and team-building games. The event creates a joyful and relaxed environment for both current and past members to reconnect, celebrate achievements, and reflect on their journey together.

  • Annual General Meeting (AGM)

    EIA conducts its Annual General Meeting (AGM) to review the year’s activities, discuss future plans, and elect new office bearers. The meeting brings together all members to reflect on achievements, share ideas, and strengthen the organization’s mission of supporting students.
